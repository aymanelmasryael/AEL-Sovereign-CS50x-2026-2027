🔬 Deconstructing Modular Sub-Routines

Rather than saturating the primary forever loop with 20 convoluted nested conditional statements, Malan introduces an exceptionally clean architectural model rooted in the Separation of Concerns. listen for keyboard handles input polling, while feel for walls immediately inverts motion vectors upon collision to prevent sprite clipping!
